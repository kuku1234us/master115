What is an AI Face Swap?
An AI Face Swap is a technology that uses artificial intelligence to automatically replace one person's face with another in photos, GIFs, or videos. It allows for seamless and realistic swapping of faces without the need for manual editing. With tools like PixNova AI Face Swap, the process becomes even easier—users can simply upload a photo or video, and in seconds, PixNova AI accurately swaps faces while maintaining the original image or video quality. This technology is often used for fun, entertainment, and creative projects, making face swaps accessible to everyone.
How to swap a face in a photo using the AI Face Swapper?
Simply upload your photo to the AI Face Swap Playground. In the "Source Image" section, upload the photo where you want to swap the face. Then, in the "Face Image" section, upload the face you want to use for the swap. Our AI tool will process both images and perform the face swap for you instantly!
Is there a time limit for video face swaps?
Of course, due to limited server resources, video face swaps currently support a maximum length of 10 seconds. As the functionality improves in the future, the supported duration for face swaps will be extended.
How many faces can be swapped in a multi-face swap?
Our face swap AI currently supports swapping up to 5 faces.
